Campbell Linker

View CV Get in touch

I'm a post graduate associate at Yale University, working as a Lab Manager in the Computational Clinical Science Lab under the mentorship of Dr. Shirley Wang. My research interests concern modeling the time scale of self-injurious thoughts and behaviors through rigorous computational methods. I'm interested in the complex role of psychiatric diagnosis, as the vast heterogeneity in diagnostic criteria and individuals' lived experiences with psychopathology present unique challenges in developing scalable models for predicting high-risk thoughts and behaviors.
